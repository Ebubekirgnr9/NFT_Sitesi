Who are we?

Hello, first of all, I would like to talk about why we started the CatZoe project and why I proceeded through the cat character in the character selection. I've always been intrigued by the calm and cute nature of cats. I am very happy to see the increasing number of stray animals support projects. I thought about how I could raise awareness on behalf of animals and decided to reflect this on my art. That's why I decided to start the CatZoe Project with my teammates. The Cat Zoe collection consists of 666 artifacts augmented by unique artificial intelligence. I am very excited to combine each piece with collectors. This project is made exclusively for Crypto.com. Thanks for your support
